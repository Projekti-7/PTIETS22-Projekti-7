About our product

The product evolved from an idea to offer a healthier alternative to sports drinks.
Shilajis have been known for centuries in northern India and Nepal, among other places, and, for example, the Himalayan
Sherpat tribe uses it regularly.
Sherpas are known as powerful and long-lived mountaineers.
BURST is a combination of this natural treasure and pure Finnish spring water. In addition, the drink contains a healthy dose of
flavouring and sweetener.

More information about Shilajit

  • Contains over 84 minerals and folic acid
  • Acts as an antioxidant in the body and improves immunity
  • A natural energy and resilience booster
